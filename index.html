I'm always looking to make the next nifty tool that will make (my) life easier, below you can see some of the projects I've worked on.

Projects

Game Bridge Reshade addon

This is a Reshade addon that enables the Simulated Reality SDK in 3D applications. This combined with a 3D shader like SuperDepth3D or a mod like Geo-11 can upgrade your gaming experience into the third dimension. It's very performant and compatible with hundreds of games.

Go to Github

Game Bridge Installer

Installer the Game Bridge ReShade addon was often a lengthy endeavour. To this end, I developed a simple C# installer that is capable of installing ReShade, the addon, SR SDK dependencies and a shader fix/mod to make a game 3D.

Parallel

A school project made with a team of 20+ people, from 2nd to 4th year and a few master students. The project was mostly focussed on the managing and communicative side of game development. The game is a 4-player coop, top-down zombie shooter with a procedurally generated map and was made in Unreal Engine. I made the camera, score tracking, split-screen and loading/spawning of the buildings using the PCG locations.
